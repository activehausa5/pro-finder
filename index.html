Recovery Mode Guide

Follow these steps to recover a phrase with 1-3 missing words:

  1. Select Wallet Profile: Choose the software or hardware wallet where the seed was originally created to ensure the correct derivation path.
  2. Enter Targets: Paste the public Ethereum address(es) you are trying to recover. The engine will stop once a match is found.
  3. Define Missing Spots: In the Missing Indices field, enter the positions (starting from 0). For example, if the 1st and 12th words are gone, enter 0, 11.
  4. Place Placeholders: In the Seed Phrase box, type your phrase and use a ? in place of the words you don't know.
  5. Adjust Performance: Slide the CPU selector to your desired intensity. Max is recommended for faster results if not using the PC for other tasks.

What is Multi-Threading?

Traditional software uses 1 "lane" of your CPU. Multi-threading opens all available "lanes" (cores) to calculate millions of seed combinations simultaneously.

Threads Description Impact
1 - 2 Standard Mode Silent fans, low power.
Half Balanced Fast recovery + usable laptop.
MAX Turbo Mode Maximum speed, high heat.

Note: Doubling threads effectively cuts recovery time in half.

The "Core Benefit"

By assigning each CPU core a specific range of the search space, Phrase Finder Pro ensures that no calculations are repeated. This linear scaling means an 24-core laptop is nearly 24x faster than a single-core process.

Reorder Mode Guide

Use this when you have all 12 words but the order is scrambled:

  • Word Bank: Paste all 12 words in any order, separated by spaces.
  • The Template: This is the most powerful tool. If you know certain words are in the correct spot, enter them. Use null for spots where you are guessing.
    Example: word1, null, null, word4...
  • Linear Scaling: Every null you replace with a known word reduces the total combinations exponentially.
